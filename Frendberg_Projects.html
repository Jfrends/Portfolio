Bridge Design Project

Program: Bridge Designer 2016
POE
March 2021

In this project, my teammates and I each made a few bridges in a bridge simulation program. The program gave us feedback on what we could improve through telling us the amount of tension or compression each of the members went through, and the goal was to make the cheapest bridge possible. It helped us learn about truss structures and the ways that different types of bridges work. My role in my team was the project manager, so I gained some important leadership and organization skills that would apply to future school and work projects.
